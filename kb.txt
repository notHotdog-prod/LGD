2. DESKMONKEY — OUR FLAGSHIP PRODUCT
============================================================

What it is:
deskMonkey is a 24/7 AI receptionist that answers a
business's phone and texts in its own brand voice. It
qualifies callers, books appointments straight into the
business's calendar, sends SMS confirmations, and emails
the owner a structured summary of every conversation.

Who it is for:
- Auto body shops (featured vertical)
- Home services: plumbing, HVAC, electrical, landscaping,
  roofing, tree service, locksmith, garage door, pool,
  pest control, septic, fence, paving
- Medical and dental offices
- Legal and professional services
- Restaurants
- Recreation, outdoor, and tourism operators
- Any local business that loses revenue to unanswered
  calls

What it does on every call:
1. Answers in your brand voice within one ring, 24/7/365.
2. Qualifies the caller (service needed, urgency,
   service address, callback number).
3. Books the appointment into your calendar directly
   (Cal.com, Google Calendar, Outlook, GoHighLevel).
4. Texts the caller a confirmation with the booking link
   or next-step instructions.
5. Emails the business owner a clean post-call summary
   (transcript, structured intake data, sentiment).
6. Detects and blocks spam and telemarketing calls
   automatically.
7. Handles emergencies: when the caller signals urgency
   (gas leak, water leak, no heat, downed tree, fender
   bender at night), it captures location and immediately
   texts the owner for a callback within minutes.

What it does NOT do:
- It does not give technical or medical diagnosis. For
  any "is my breaker tripped" or "is this covered by my
  insurance" question, it politely books a visit. Bad
  advice causes real harm, and a booked visit beats a
  dodged question.
- It does not replace the business owner. The owner's
  phone rings first. deskMonkey only picks up if the
  owner does not answer, declines, or is busy.
- It does not deceive callers. We do not pretend to be
  human; we sound natural and competent, and we handle
  the call without making the caller jump through hoops.

How it works (setup):
1. Keep your existing business number. Nothing changes
   for your customers.
2. Forward unanswered calls to deskMonkey. That is a
   30-second setting on your phone or carrier portal,
   and we walk you through it.
3. We train deskMonkey on your business in under a week:
   services, pricing, hours, FAQs, intake questions,
   your voice and tone.
4. Go live. Watch your missed-call list become a
   booked-appointment list.

Time to launch: 3 to 5 business days for most clients.
The bottleneck is usually collecting business details
from the owner, not the build.

Built on: Trillet (white-label voice AI infrastructure).
The brain, prompt, integrations, and brand are all Lets
Grow Digital. The underlying telephony and speech stack
is best-in-class infrastructure we resell.

Compliance posture: HIPAA, SOC 2 Type II, TCPA, and GDPR
compliant out of the box. We sign Business Associate
Agreements (BAAs) for medical and dental clients. See
section 6 for details.

6. COMPLIANCE AND SECURITY
============================================================

HIPAA: deskMonkey is HIPAA compliant. We sign Business
Associate Agreements (BAAs) for medical and dental
clients. PHI is encrypted in transit and at rest, and
all transcripts and recordings are stored on
HIPAA-eligible infrastructure.

SOC 2 Type II: The underlying voice AI infrastructure
holds an active SOC 2 Type II audit covering security,
availability, and confidentiality.

TCPA (Telephone Consumer Protection Act): All outbound
calling features respect TCPA consent rules. We do not
make outbound robocalls without prior express written
consent. Our Meta Lead Form callback flow uses the
consent captured in the lead form itself.

A2P 10DLC (SMS): Lets Grow Digital is registered with
The Campaign Registry (TCR) for A2P 10DLC SMS. Our
SMS message templates are submitted, approved, and
include required opt-out language. Inbound forms on
letsgrowdigital.ai capture explicit SMS consent with
a TCPA-compliant checkbox before any SMS is sent.

GDPR: For European-resident contacts, we support data
access, portability, and deletion requests through
hello@letsgrowdigital.ai. We do not currently market
to or solicit European businesses.

Privacy policy:      https://letsgrowdigital.ai/privacy.html
Terms of service:    https://letsgrowdigital.ai/terms.html

Data handling principles:
- API tokens, API keys, and CRM credentials live only
  in server-side Cloudflare Workers, never in
  client-side code.
- Call transcripts are retained per the client's data
  retention policy (default: 90 days).
- Recordings can be turned off per number on request.
- All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.2+) and at
  rest (AES-256).
