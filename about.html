Our Story

Built for Growth-Minded Companies

Lets Grow Digital was founded by Bryan and Kirk — two operators tired of digital marketing agencies that are slow, expensive, and behind on technology.

We're building something different: a founder-led shop that combines human strategic thinking with cutting-edge AI, moves fast, measures everything, and stays obsessed with your ROI — not our billable hours.

Our focus is on ambitious local businesses where AI-native execution wins — auto body shops, home service pros, and trades-driven companies that depend on every inbound call. Every strategy we deploy is informed by data, powered by AI, and relentlessly focused on moving the needle on revenue.
