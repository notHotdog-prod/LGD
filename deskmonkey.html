How It Works

Four steps. Every call. Same brain.

deskMonkey follows the same sales-floor playbook a great front-desk hire would — without the lunch breaks, sick days, or shift changes.

1

Identify

Greets the caller by your business name, captures their name and callback number, confirms the digits back.

2

Screen

One open-ended "what's going on?" Lets the customer describe the job. Detects spam and telemarketers and politely ends those calls.

3

Qualify

Classifies urgency (emergency, same-day, standard, quote-only), captures service address, asks the trade-specific intake questions you set up.

4

Book

Offers the next slot from your calendar, books it, sends SMS confirmation, emails you a summary. Done — before the next call rings.
