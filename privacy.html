Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 19, 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you work with us or visit our site, we collect:

  • Business name, contact information, and industry details
  • Marketing goals, target audience, and campaign objectives
  • Email, phone, and business address
  • Financial and payment information (processed securely)
  • Performance data and campaign analytics
  • Website usage data via cookies and analytics
  • IP address, browser type, and device information

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Develop and execute digital marketing strategies
  • Manage campaigns across SEO, paid media, email, and social
  • Track performance and provide detailed reporting
  • Send invoices, updates, and campaign notifications
  • Improve our services and user experience
  • Comply with legal and regulatory obligations

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell or trade your business data. We may share information with:

  • Advertising platforms (Google Ads, Facebook, etc.) to run campaigns
  • Email service providers for newsletter and campaign delivery
  • Analytics providers to measure performance
  • Hosting and cloud service providers for infrastructure
  • Payment processors for billing
  • Legal authorities when required by law

4. Cookies and Tracking

Our website uses cookies and similar technologies to remember your preferences, understand how you use our site, and measure the effectiveness of our digital marketing. You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect site functionality and your experience.

5. Your Privacy Rights

Under New Jersey privacy laws and federal regulations, you have the right to:

  • Request access to your personal information
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Opt out of promotional communications
  • Request a portable copy of your data
  • Object to certain data processing activities

To exercise these rights, contact us at hello@letsgrowdigital.ai or 732-484-3000.

6. Data Security

We implement encryption, secure servers, firewalls, and strict access controls to protect your information. We follow industry best practices for security. However, no system is completely secure. We encourage strong passwords and immediate reporting of suspicious activity.

7. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Our services are for business owners and marketing professionals, not children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If we discover a child has provided information, we will delete it promptly. Parents or guardians may request removal by contacting us.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Changes will be posted with an updated effective date, and your continued use of our services indicates acceptance.
