Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2026

This website is operated by GBBB Holdings LLC, doing business as Lets Grow Digital.

When you work with us or visit our site, we collect:

  • Business name, contact information, and industry details
  • Marketing goals, target audience, and campaign objectives
  • Email, phone, and business address
  • Financial and payment information (processed securely)
  • Performance data and campaign analytics
  • Website usage data via cookies and analytics
  • IP address, browser type, and device information

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Develop and execute digital marketing strategies
  • Manage campaigns across SEO, paid media, email, and social
  • Track performance and provide detailed reporting
  • Send invoices, updates, and campaign notifications
  • Improve our services and user experience
  • Comply with legal and regulatory obligations

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell or trade your business data. We may share information with:

  • Advertising platforms (Google Ads, Facebook, etc.) to run campaigns
  • Email service providers for newsletter and campaign delivery
  • Analytics providers to measure performance
  • Hosting and cloud service providers for infrastructure
  • Payment processors for billing
  • Legal authorities when required by law

4. SMS and Text Messaging Privacy

If you opt in to receive SMS or text messages from Lets Grow Digital, the following applies in addition to the rest of this Privacy Policy:

No sharing of mobile information. We do not sell, rent, share, or otherwise disclose your mobile phone number, SMS opt-in consent records, or any data related to your SMS interactions with us to any third parties or affiliates for their marketing or promotional purposes. This restriction applies regardless of the third party's identity or relationship to us. Mobile information is not shared with third parties for marketing under any circumstances.

What we collect for SMS: Mobile phone number, opt-in source (contact form, Cal.com booking, or in-person), timestamp, IP address at time of opt-in, and message content/timestamps for messages sent and received.

How we use it: Solely to send the appointment confirmations, appointment reminders, and customer service messages you have opted in to receive, and to maintain compliance records as required by federal and state law.

Sub-processors. SMS delivery is handled by Twilio Inc. and our configured messaging service providers, who process this data only to deliver messages on our behalf and are contractually prohibited from using it for any other purpose.

Your rights. You may opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message. You may request deletion of your phone number and SMS consent records by emailing hello@letsgrowdigital.ai or calling 732-484-3000.

Our website uses cookies and similar technologies to remember your preferences, understand how you use our site, and measure the effectiveness of our digital marketing. You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect site functionality and your experience.

To exercise these rights, contact us at hello@letsgrowdigital.ai or 732-484-3000.
